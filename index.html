Valais Hiking Explorer, Where Passion Meets Trails

Inspired by my deep passion for hiking and my admiration of the majestic mountains of Valais, I developed the Valais Hiking Explorer as my personal project during an intensive one-month challenge at the Powercoders bootcamp. Leveraging Node.js, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I tackled the engaging challenge of integrating open-source hiking trail data APIs into an intuitive and interactive platform. This project enabled hikers to effortlessly find and explore trails through dynamic filters, downloadable maps, and comprehensive route details. It perfectly blended my enthusiasm for outdoor adventures with my growing technical skills.
